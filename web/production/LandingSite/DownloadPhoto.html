Download Photo

You can download your own personal photos or friends' photos from Prefiniti.

Click on the photo you wish to download either from your own photos or your friend's photos (step 1). Select 'View File' (step 2). Your browser will open a new window.

A full size image will appear in the 'File Preview' area. Click on the 'Download This File' button for a direct link to the photo (step 3).
