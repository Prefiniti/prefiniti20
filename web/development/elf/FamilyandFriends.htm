Family:

Even though I said that I pretty much hated my family at times,
there are some really good times that I wouldn't change for the world either.


My Grandmother:
I don't think I would ever be able to get along with my entire family without my late grandmother.
My real fathers' mother was the glue of the family and she tried to keep everything in order.
I defiantly think that I've learned the most from her. She would sit with all her grandchildren,
and just talk to them as if they were adults. That's what I loved about her, I felt older.
I feel like there are a lot of things that I wouldn't be able to get through without the thought
of her just talking things out. When she passed, I felt as if I lost a little bit of myself.
I lost some wisdom and courage, but knowing that her sprit is still around makes me feel better.
She really taught me that it's okay to be who you want to be, no matter who you want to be.

My mother can be very opinionative, which gets really annoying and we fight a lot because of it.
But I do have to say, that I love my mother, because I wouldn't be here if it wasn't for her.
