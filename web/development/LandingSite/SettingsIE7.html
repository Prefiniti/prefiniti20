Adjust your settings for Prefiniti compatibility in Internet Explorer
Before you begin, please make sure you have installed Internet Explorer 7. Click here to verify your version of Explorer and download IE7 if necessary.


Prefiniti requires the use of Java. Click here to check if you already have Java or to install Java for free.

 

Part A: Enable JavaScript


In Internet Explorer 7 click on the 'Tools' button (step 1) located in the upper right part of the screen (next to the gear icon) and select 'Internet Options' (step 2).



Click on the 'Security' tab (step 3) and then click on the 'Custom Level...' button (step 4).



Scroll down (step 5) until you can see options as shown on the picture. They are located near the bottom of the list. Set the option 'Active scripting' to 'Enable' (step 6). Then click on the 'OK' button (step 7).



Click 'Yes' (step 8).



Click 'OK' (step 9). This concludes enabling JavaScript for Microsoft Internet Explorer 7.


 

Part B: Enable Java


In Internet Explorer 7 click on the 'Tools' button (step 1) located in the upper right part of the screen (next to the gear icon) and select 'Internet Options' (step 2).



Click on the ‘Security’ tab (step 3). Scroll down (step 4) until you can see ‘Java (Sun)’ as shown on the picture. Put a check in the box ‘Use JRE (requires restart)’ (step 5). Then click on the 'OK' button (step 6). Then you must close and restart Explorer.



 

Part C: Check for new content every time a page is visited


In Internet Explorer 7 click on the 'Tools' button (step 1) located in the upper right part of the screen (next to the gear icon) and select 'Internet Options' (step 2).



Click on the ‘General’ tab (step 3). Under ‘Browsing history’ click the ‘Settings’ button (step 4).



Select the option ‘Every time I visit the webpage’ (step 5). Click ‘OK’ (step 6).



Click ‘OK’ (step 7).
